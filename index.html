CV

I am currently enroled as a PhD candidate at University of Utrecht, the Netherlands. My project is somewhere along the lines of structure-aware version control systems, supervised by Wouter Swierstra.

Our goal is to develop a datatype-generic approach to diffing, in Agda. Having a solid and formal notion of diffing should let us also tackle the problem of merging structured data, which lieas at the heart of the difficulties for version control.
